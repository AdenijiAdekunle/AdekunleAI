About

Hi! I'm Adekunle Adeniji, a Data Analyst. I leverage my strong analytical skills to extract meaningful insights from complex datasets, bridging the gap between language and numbers. With a background in English and Literary Studies, I bring a unique perspective to data analysis, effectively communicating data-driven insights to various audiences. My meticulous attention to detail and problem-solving abilities enable me to unravel intricate data puzzles and empower organizations to make informed decisions. My journey from literature to data analysis underscores my adaptability and commitment to lifelong learning, as I strive to make impactful contributions in my field.

    My Skill set includes:
  • Programming: Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Visualization: Excel, Tableau, Power Bi, Seaborn, Matplotlib
  • Other Tools: Numpy, Pandas, Microsoft Office Suite, NLTK
